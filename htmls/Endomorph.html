Endomoprh

Endomorphs have softer bodies with curves. They have a wide waist and hips and large bones, though they may or may not be overweight. Their weight is often in their hips, thighs, and lower abdomen

This solution is a complex of properly selected exercises, nutrition, and rest. Otherwise, the desired result can not be achieved.

Advantages

Greater strength potential due to higher muscle mass

Enhanced ability to store energy as fat, which can be beneficial in endurance activities

Aesthetic appearance and pronounced musculature

Disadvantages

Tend to gain weight easily

Hard to lose weight due to slow metabolisms

They gain fat easily and have a thicker bone structure
